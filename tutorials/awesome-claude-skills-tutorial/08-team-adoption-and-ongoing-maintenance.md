# Chapter 8: Team Adoption and Ongoing Maintenance

This chapter covers operationalizing skills at team scale.

## Learning Goals

- define approved baseline skill sets
- establish review cadence for drift and deprecations
- keep internal docs synchronized with actual usage
- measure outcome improvements over time

## Team Operations Checklist

- maintain an approved core skill baseline
- assign skill owners for high-impact automations
- prune low-value or risky skills regularly
- track quality, velocity, and incident impact metrics
